Black Rye Bread Recipe

Description

Black Rye Bread is popular bread made in Russian culture.
It has a very earthy taste to it.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Water
  • 2 tsps Cider Vinegar
  • 2 Cups Flour
  • 1 Cup Rye Flour
  • 2 teaspoons dark cornsyrup
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tsp dry yeast



Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F degrees
  2. Combine ingredients in mixing bowl
  3. Place in tray and bake for an hour and a half
